WALLET20m▶
Your wallet isn't really a wallet — it holds private keys. The money is on the blockchain. Whoever has the key controls the funds.
🔐Private Key = key to the vault. Public Key = account number. Address = address. Seed Phrase = backup of all your keys.
WHAT YOU'LL LEARN
Hot Wallet vs Cold Wallet — connected to internet or not
Custodial vs Non-custodial — who holds the key?
Seed Phrases — BIP-39, 12/24 words
Private Key → Public Key → Address — derivation chain
MetaMask, Ledger, Trezor — common tools
HOT
Hot Wallet: MetaMask, Trust Wallet. Connected to net — convenient but vulnerable. For daily use.
COLD
Cold Wallet: Ledger, Trezor. Offline — private key stays on device. $100+ = store on cold.
SEED
BIP-39: 12 or 24 English words = backup of all keys. "abandon ability able..." → write on paper, never in cloud.
DERIVE
Private Key (256 random bits) → Public Key (ECC secp256k1) → Address (hash + checksum). One-way — can't reverse.
💡FTX Collapse (2022): Millions put money in custodial wallet (FTX). When it collapsed — everything vanished. "Not your keys, not your crypto."
